About RCOA

The Rice-Crab Optimization Algorithm (RCOA) is a nature-inspired metaheuristic optimization algorithm that combines exploration and exploitation strategies inspired by rice growth patterns and crab foraging behavior.

This collection of interactive demos showcases various applications of the RCOA algorithm in solving real-world optimization problems across different domains.

For more information, check out the original research paper, the skeptical peer review, and the revised paper addressing identified gaps.

Validation: Run node verify-metrics.js for statistical validation (51 runs with Wilcoxon signed-rank test), or node demo-fleet-headless.js for quick benchmarking.
